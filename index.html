About Me

Altschool ID: ALT/SOE/024/0957

My name is Jacinth David. I am a 23 year old graduate of Covenant University where studied Electrical Electronics Engineering.I was born on March 28, 2001, emerging as the firstborn of four siblings, a position that has shaped my leadership skills and creative drive from an early age. Growing up in a bustling household, I quickly learned the art of self-expression, finding my voice through various artistic mediums. My passion for creation manifested initially through drawing and sketching, but it wasn't until I discovered 2D animation that I truly found my calling.My journey in animation began as a hobby during my teenage years, experimenting with simple software and creating short clips that caught the attention of friends and family. As I honed my skills, my unique style began to emerge – a blend of vibrant colors, fluid movements, and storytelling that spoke to my Nigerian heritage and contemporary influences. This dedication to my craft eventually led to collaborations with notable figures in the Nigerian music and entertainment industry. My work with Victony, known for his Afro-fusion sound, involved creating animated music videos that perfectly captured the essence of his music.
The collaboration with Korty, a prominent content creator, saw me developing animated intros and transitions for her YouTube channel, adding a distinctive flair to her already popular content. My animations for Azanti helped visualize his musical narratives, while my work with Wonu Osikoya brought her vision for Afrobeats storytelling to life in animated form.These projects not only showcased my talent but also exposed me to the business side of creative work, planting the seeds for my future entrepreneurial endeavors. As my reputation grew, so did my ambition to push the boundaries of my craft.In 2023, a pivotal year in my career, I made the bold decision to expand my skill set into the realm of software development. This move was driven by a desire to understand the technical backbone of the digital tools I used daily in my animation work. Starting as a product manager, I gained valuable insights into the development process, user experience design, and project management – skills that would prove invaluable in my future pursuits.However, my curiosity didn't stop there. Recognizing the growing importance of cloud technology and backend systems in modern software applications, I pivoted towards cloud engineering and backend development. This transition wasn't just about learning new programming languages or cloud platforms; it was about envisioning how these technologies could revolutionize the animation industry.As I delved deeper into software development, I began to see parallels between coding and animation. Both required attention to detail, logical thinking, and the ability to bring abstract concepts to life.
This realization fueled my determination to find ways to merge these two passions.My educational background played a crucial role in shaping my multifaceted approach to problem-solving. During high school, my involvement in the Sustainable Development Club, a Shell initiative, exposed me to real-world challenges and the power of innovation in addressing them. Participating in competitions across Rivers State not only honed my teamwork and presentation skills but also instilled in me a deep appreciation for sustainable solutions.These experiences laid the groundwork for my impressive performance at the inaugural Sync hackathon in January 2024. Leading my team to a second-place finish, I demonstrated my ability to conceptualize, develop, and present an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) within the challenging 48-hour timeframe. This achievement was more than just a competition win; it was a defining moment that validated my unique blend of creative and technical skills.The success at the Sync hackathon ignited a new ambition within me – to create software products that solve real-world problems, particularly in the creative industry. I began conceptualizing a cloud-based platform that would streamline the animation workflow, allowing artists to collaborate seamlessly across distances, render complex animations using distributed computing power, and integrate with various digital asset management systems.My vision for the future is ambitious yet grounded in my diverse experiences. I dream of opening an animation studio that's not just a creative powerhouse but also a tech innovator. This studio would serve as a hub for animators, software developers, and storytellers to collaborate on groundbreaking projects. I envision developing proprietary software tools that leverage cloud technology and machine learning to automate time-consuming aspects of animation, allowing artists to focus more on creativity and less on technical hurdles.Moreover, my background in sustainable development has inspired me to ensure that my future ventures have a positive impact on society and the environment. I'm exploring ways to make the animation industry more sustainable, from reducing the carbon footprint of rendering farms to creating educational content that raises awareness about environmental issues through engaging animated storytelling.
As I continue to develop my skills in both animation and software engineering, I'm also focusing on building a network of like-minded individuals who share my vision of merging art and technology. I've started mentoring young animators, introducing them to basic coding concepts, and encouraging them to think beyond traditional animation techniques.My journey is a testament to the power of interdisciplinary thinking in the modern world. By bridging the gap between the creative and technical realms, I'm positioning myself at the forefront of a new wave of digital creators who are equally comfortable with a stylus and a command line.Looking ahead, I'm excited about the possibilities that emerging technologies like augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) hold for animation. I'm already experimenting with ways to bring my 2D animations into 3D spaces, creating immersive experiences that push the boundaries of storytelling.As I approach my mid-twenties, I stand at the cusp of a promising career that defies traditional boundaries. My unique blend of artistic talent, technical knowledge, and entrepreneurial spirit sets me apart in an increasingly competitive digital landscape. Whether I'm animating a music video, coding a new feature for my animation software, or pitching my startup idea to investors, I bring the same level of passion and creativity to every endeavor.My story is still unfolding, but one thing is clear: my journey from a young animation enthusiast to a multifaceted creator and innovator is just the beginning. With my determination to push the boundaries of what's possible in animation and software development, I'm not just adapting to the future of digital creation – I'm actively shaping it.
